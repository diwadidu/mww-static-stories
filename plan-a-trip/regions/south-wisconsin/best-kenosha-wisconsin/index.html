Trip Tips: Kenosha

Trails are proliferating everywhere, and they're not always state trails. In Wisconsin, much of the action is in cities and counties. Kenosha, for example, has 11 miles of trails along Lake Michigan, which connect to 10 miles of lakefront trail in Racine.

Rustic Road Brewing Co. downtown.

Hawthorn Hollow Nature Sanctuary and Arboretum.

Kenosha Harbor Market on Saturday mornings.

Bicycling: The 18.6-mile Kenosha County Bike Trail heads to the Illinois border, where it meets the 26.5-mile Robert McClory Bike Path to Highland Park.

On the north it connects to Racine County via the three-mile North Shore Trail.

Kenosha, Kenosha History Center. In addition to the Nash, Kenosha made Ramblers, Hudsons and AMC cars and trucks between 1902 and 1988. Its Rambler Legacy Gallery shows rotating exhibits.
