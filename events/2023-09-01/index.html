<Friday, September 1, 2023>

  1. Minnesota State Fair

    St. Paul, MN2023-09-01

    Try flights at the Craft Beer Hall, eat food on a stick, visit the Miracle of Birth barn, listen to free concerts and watch the 2 p.m. daily parade.

    1. Event website

  2. Midwest Old Threshers Reunion

    Mount Pleasant, IA2023-09-01

    This gathering of steam engines and antique cars, trucks and tractors also features a horse pull, concerts, square dancing and a parade.

    1. Event website

  3. Chicago Jazz Festival

    Chicago, IL2023-09-01

    This free festival features music at the Chicago Cultural Center and Millennium Park plus other venues.

    1. Event website

  4. Western Minnesota Steam Threshers Reunion

    Rollag, MN2023-09-01

    Revel in the past during this show of antique machinery, which includes rides on trains and a carousel, square dancing, roving musicians and nightly jamborees.

    1. Event website

  5. Wisconsin State Cow Chip Throw

    Prairie du Sac, WI2023-09-01

    There's chip-chucking contests in this Wisconsin River town, but also music, kids' games and an arts and crafts fair. 

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  6. Marquette Area Blues Fest

    Marquette, MI2023-09-01

    Enjoy a weekend of music in Mattson Lower Harbor Park on Lake Superior.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends Marquette story

  7. Wilhelm Tell Festival

    New Glarus, WI2023-09-01

    This Swiss town near Madison has been putting on an outdoor William Tell pageant since 1938. Festivities include a children's lantern parade, yodeling, an arts show and ethnic fashions.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  8. Wisconsin Highland Games

    Waukesha, WI2023-09-01

    In addition to heavy athletics — throwing weights, hammers, logs and sacks of hay — there will be piping, drumming, dancing, longbow competitions, dog demonstrations and traditional Scottish food.

    1. Event website

  9. Red Cliff Cultural Days

    Bayfield, WI2023-09-01

    At this Ojibwe community just north of Bayfield, there will be a veterans' powwow, frybread-eating contest, canoe and kayak races, music and fireworks.
