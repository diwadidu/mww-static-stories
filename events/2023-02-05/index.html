  4. Groundhog Days

    Woodstock, IL2023-02-05

    The 1993 movie "Groundhog Day'' was filmed in this town an hour northwest of Chicago, and it celebrates with trivia, a bags tourney, bingo and walking tours of movie sites, including the 1889 Opera House, where actor Bill Murray jumped from the belfry. At 7 a.m. Thursday, Woodstock Willie will look for his shadow.

    1. Event website
