<Friday, August 11, 2023>

  1. Grant Park Music Festival

    Chicago, IL2023-08-11

    Professional musicians, many of them world-famous, offer free concerts nearly every evening in Millennium Park's beautiful Pritzker Pavilion. This year's schedule includes favorites by Beethoven, Gershwin and Tchaikovsky as well as choral, circus and Broadway evenings.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends stories

  2. Folklorama

    Winnipeg, MB2023-08-11

    This Manitoba prairie town celebrates its heritage with pavilions representing 44 nationalities, offering music, dance and food at many venues.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  3. Wisconsin State Fair

    West Allis, WI2023-08-11

    The fair in this Milwaukee suburb features big-name musicians, a sea-lion show, a daily parade and lots of contests, including hog-calling, cookie-stacking and cream puff-eating.

    1. Event website

  4. Cache Ba$h

    West Bend, WI2023-08-11

    This town north of Milwaukee calls itself the Geocache Capital of the Midwest. For the festival, it hides 50 new caches and gives away $1,000 in cash prizes.

    1. Event website

  5. Live From the Rock

    Red Rock, ON2023-08-11

    This folk festival is on the shores of Lake Superior, east of Thunder Bay near Nipigon. Camping is on-site.

    1. Event website

  6. Iowa State Fair

    Des Moines, IA2023-08-11

    See a diaper derby, outhouse races and mullet contest, among many other contests, and don't miss the butter sculptures.

    1. Event website

  7. Minnesota Bluegrass & Old-Time Music Festival

    Richmond, MN2023-08-11

    This family-friendly festival includes music on five stages and workshops at El Rancho Mañana Campground, 20 miles west of St. Cloud. 

    1. Event website

  8. National Blueberry Festival

    South Haven, MI2023-08-11

    Eat until you're blue in the face during the festival in this Lake Michigan beach town. There's a pie-eating contest, riverfront concerts, an arts fair and a big parade on Saturday.

    1. Event website

  9. Ashley for the Arts music festival

    Arcadia, WI2023-08-11

    This nonprofit festival is in the southwest Wisconsin bluffs, half an hour north of Winona, Minn. Trace Adkins and Tyler Hubbard are among the headliners, which include a variety of country and rock stars. Thursday admission is free, and Friday-Saturday tickets are $20-$30. In addition to music, there will be an arts fair, children's activities and Saturday fireworks.

    1. Event website

  10. Grand Portage Powwow & Rendezvous

    Grand Portage, MN2023-08-11

    Re-enactors from across the continent gather at Grand Portage National Monument for the annual fur-trade rendezvous; next door, the  Grand Portage Band of Lake Superior Chippewa holds a powwow. Reserve lodgings far in advance.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  11. Bayfront Blues Festival

    Duluth, MN2023-08-11

    The annual music fest on the Lake Superior harbor showcases national and regional acts on three stages.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends Duluth story

  12. Irish Fair

    St. Paul, MN2023-08-11

    This big heritage fest on the banks of the Mississippi includes Irish Got Talent and best legs in a kilt contests, hurling demonstrations, dancing and lots of music.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  13. Quad Cities Balloon Festival

    East Moline, IL2023-08-11

    This festival at the Rock Island County Fairgrounds includes balloon glows, launches, tethered rides and a play area for children.

    1. Event website

  14. Shawano Folk Music Festival

    Shawano, WI2023-08-11

    The free, family-friendly festival in this northwoods town includes dance, crafts, children's activities and instruction in Franklin Park.

    1. Event website

  15. Tug Fest

    LeClaire and Port Byron, IA/IL2023-08-11

    The two towns stretch a 680-pound, 2,400-foot rope across the Mississippi River for the ultimate tug of war. There's also a parade, fireworks, children's games, concerts and carnival rides. Score so far: Illinois 24, Iowa 11.

    1. Event website/LeClaire
    2. Event website/Port Byron

  16. Grand Marais Music Festival

    Grand Marais, MI2023-08-11

    In addition to music, there will be food and arts and crafts vendors.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  17. Sweet Corn Festival

    Mendota, IL2023-08-11

    The festival in this town between Rochelle and La Salle features nearly 50 tons of free buttered corn, live music, a flea market, a carnival and a parade.
