<Friday, July 14, 2023>

  1. Grant Park Music Festival

    Chicago, IL2023-07-14

    Professional musicians, many of them world-famous, offer free concerts nearly every evening in Millennium Park's beautiful Pritzker Pavilion. This year's schedule includes favorites by Beethoven, Gershwin and Tchaikovsky as well as choral, circus and Broadway evenings.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends stories

  2. Great Rendezvous Celebration

    Thunder Bay, ON2023-07-14

    This festival at Fort William Historical Park is part of 50th anniversary celebrations and includes a historic fur-trade reenactment the second weekend.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends Thunder Bay story

  3. Fiesta Days

    McHenry, IL2023-07-14

    There's a Music Fest, carnival, beach bash, car show and fireworks in Petersen Park the first weekend. The second weekend, there's an art and street fair Saturday and a 1:30 p.m. Sunday parade on Main Street.

    1. Event website

  4. Laura Ingalls Wilder pageant

    Walnut Grove, MN2023-07-14

    The festival in this western Minnesota town revolves around the book "On the Banks of Plum Creek.'' 

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  5. Northwest Water Carnival

    Detroit Lakes, MN2023-07-14

    This big festival celebrates its 87th year with a sand-castle contest, water fights, ship-building (in less than 30 minutes), beer tasting, watermelon feed, bike tour and more. On the last day, there's a big parade at 1 p.m.

    1. Event website

  6. Paperfest

    Kimberly, WI2023-07-14

    This Fox Cities town celebrates its heritage with toilet-paper tosses, lawn games, human foosball, a firefighter tug of war and 35 bands on three stages in Sunset Park.

    1. Event website

  7. Riverfest

    Monticello, MN2023-07-14

    This Mississippi River town celebrates summer with a car show, pie-eating contest, Friday fireworks and noon parade on Sunday.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  8. Rock Fest

    Cadott, WI2023-07-14

    This outdoor festival is near Chippewa Falls. There's onsite camping.

    1. Event website

  9. Lumberjack Days

    Stillwater, MN2023-07-14

    This music festival along the St. Croix River also features lumberjack shows and such attractions as a downhill derby, hammerschlagen, helicopter rides and a parade. 

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  10. Sinclair Lewis Days

    Sauk Centre, MN2023-07-14

    The hometown of the Nobel Prize-winning author celebrates with such events as turtle races, bicycle stunts, Friday fireworks, a craft fair, music and a parade Saturday.

    1. Event website

  11. Bastille Days

    Milwaukee, WI2023-07-14

    Look for minstrels, cancan dancers, French lessons, a Mardi Gras parade and a 43-foot replica of the Eiffel Tower in Cathedral Square Park.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends Milwaukee stories

  12. RiverSong Music Festival

    Hutchinson, MN2023-07-14

    This family-friendly festival on the Crow River, west of the Twin Cities, features folk, blues, jazz and other grassroots music on three stages, including one for children. There's onsite camping and food.

    1. Event website

  13. Honor the Earth Pow-wow

    Hayward, WI2023-07-14

    The Lac Courte Oreilles Band of Lake Superior Chippewa holds one of the largest powwows in North America, with dancing, singing and crafts plus a cooking contest, run/walk and softball tournament. 

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  14. Wannigan Days

    St. Croix Falls, WI2023-07-14

    St. Croix Falls and its cross-river neighbor, Taylors Falls, pay tribute to lumberjacks and river pigs with rock and country music, an arts and crafts fair, a Saturday parade and fireworks.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story
