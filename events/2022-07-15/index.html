  1. Grant Park Music Festival

    Chicago, IL2022-07-15

    Professional musicians, many of them world-famous, offer free concerts nearly every night in Millennium Park's beautiful Pritzker Pavilion. This year's schedule includes favorites by Beethoven, Mozart and Schubert as well as gospel, mariachi, choral, circus and Broadway evenings.

  3. Northwest Water Carnival

    Detroit Lakes, MN2022-07-15

    This big festival celebrates its 86th year with a sand-castle contest, water fights, ship-building (in less than 30 minutes), beer tasting, watermelon feed, bike tour and more. On the last day, there's a big parade at 1 p.m.

    1. Event website

    Hutchinson, MN2022-07-15

    This family-friendly festival on the Crow River, west of the Twin Cities, features folk, blues, jazz and other grassroots music on three stages, including one for children. There's onsite camping and food.

    The Lac Courte Oreilles Band of Lake Superior Chippewa holds the 50th anniversary of one of the largest powwows in North America, with dancing, singing and crafts plus a cooking contest, run/walk and softball tournament.
