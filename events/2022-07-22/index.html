  1. Grant Park Music Festival

    Chicago, IL2022-07-22

    Professional musicians, many of them world-famous, offer free concerts nearly every night in Millennium Park's beautiful Pritzker Pavilion. This year's schedule includes favorites by Beethoven, Mozart and Schubert as well as gospel, mariachi, choral, circus and Broadway evenings.

  2. Laura Ingalls Wilder pageant

    Walnut Grove, MN2022-07-22

    The festival in this western Minnesota town revolves around the book "On the Banks of Plum Creek.'' 

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  3. Charlevoix Venetian Festival

    Charlevoix, MI2022-07-22

    A week of music and games winds up with an 11 a.m. street parade and, at dusk, a lighted boat parade followed by fireworks.

    1. Event website

  4. Festa Italiana

    Milwaukee, WI2022-07-22

    At the lakefront Henry Maier Festival Park, there's cannoli-eating and pizza-making contests, celebrity chefs, a bocce ball tournament, parades at 5 p.m. Saturday and Sunday, nightly fireworks and music and dance on five stages and an opera tent.

    1. Event website

  5. Aquatennial

    Minneapolis, MN2022-07-22

    The City of Lakes holds a Wednesday Torchlight Parade, water-ski shows and Saturday fireworks.

    1. Event website

  6. Country Jam

    Eau Claire, WI2022-07-22

    Little Big Town, Dustin Lynch and Cody Johnson are  among the headliners of this fest. There's camping on-site.

    1. Event website

  7. La Crosse Storytelling Festival

    La Crosse, WI2022-07-22

    Listen to local, regional and national storytellers spin tales at the Pump House. There will also be music and arts and crafts.

    2. MidwestWeekends story

  8. Hiawatha Traditional Music Festival

    Marquette, MI2022-07-22

    This folk-music festival in Tourist Park also features a juried arts and crafts show and camping on-site.

  9. Muskie Days

    Nevis, MN2022-07-22

    The festival in this lake town on the Heartland State Trail features an inflatable obstacle course, hula-hoop instruction, a casting contest, music on three stages, kids' games, an ice cream-eating contest and a parade  Saturday.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  10. Great Folle Avoine Fur Trade Rendezvous

    Danbury, WI2022-07-22

    At this rendezvous at a rebuilt post on the banks of the Yellow River, visit with costumed traders and voyageurs from the fur-trade era and see demonstrations of tomahawk-throwing, black-powder shooting, candlemaking and other skills. There will also be a candy cannon, bead pile and kids' games. 

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  11. Maple-syrup festivals

    Around the region, 2022-07-22

    At nature centers around the region, tour the sugarbush and learn how to tap trees and boil sap. Then, eat a pancake breakfast with real maple syrup.

    1. MidwestWeekends story
