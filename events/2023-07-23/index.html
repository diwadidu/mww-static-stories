<Sunday, July 23, 2023>

  1. Grant Park Music Festival

    Chicago, IL2023-07-23

    Professional musicians, many of them world-famous, offer free concerts nearly every evening in Millennium Park's beautiful Pritzker Pavilion. This year's schedule includes favorites by Beethoven, Gershwin and Tchaikovsky as well as choral, circus and Broadway evenings.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends stories

  2. Country Thunder Wisconsin Music Festival

    Twin Lakes, WI2023-07-23

    The country-music festival between Lake Geneva and Kenosha features Jon Pardi, Jason Aldean and Keith Urban. There's camping onsite.

    1. Event website

  3. Fiesta Days

    McHenry, IL2023-07-23

    There's a Music Fest, carnival, beach bash, car show and fireworks in Petersen Park the first weekend. The second weekend, there's an art and street fair Saturday and a 1:30 p.m. Sunday parade on Main Street.

    1. Event website

  4. Northwest Water Carnival

    Detroit Lakes, MN2023-07-23

    This big festival celebrates its 87th year with a sand-castle contest, water fights, ship-building (in less than 30 minutes), beer tasting, watermelon feed, bike tour and more. On the last day, there's a big parade at 1 p.m.

    1. Event website

  5. Bavarian Blast

    New Ulm, MN2023-07-23

    In addition to German music, food and beer, this festival at the Brown County Fair Grounds features stein-holding, barrel-rolling and sauerkraut-eating contests, plus a Sunday parade.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  6. Rivertown Days

    Hastings, MN2023-07-23

    In this river town south of St. Paul, there will be 25 bands on four stages, plus a carnival, arts & crafts fair and classic-car show.

    1. Event website

  7. Midwest Fire Fest

    Cambridge, WI2023-07-23

    This pottery town east of Madison celebrates the arts fueled by fire, including clay, glass and metal. It includes fire dancing, music from seven bands, demonstrations and food, including wood-fired pizza. Fest-goers can design their own tiles to be created during a hot-iron pour Saturday.

    1. Event website

  8. Air and Water Show

    Milwaukee, WI2023-07-23

    This show, between Bradford and McKinley beaches on the lakefront, features the Blue Angels in the air and ski teams on the water.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends Milwaukee stories

  9. Water Days

    New London, MN2023-07-23

    In this lakes town near Sibley State Park, there's a water-ski show, parade, kids' fishing derby and fireworks.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  10. Pitchfork Music Festival

    Chicago, IL2023-07-23

    This indie and alt-rock festival is in Union Park. 

    1. Event website

  11. Wenonga Days

    Battle Lake, MN2023-07-23

    The festival in this lake-resort town near Glendalough State Park features walking tours, human foosball, turtle races, Civil War reenactors, a water-ski show and a parade at 7 p.m. Saturday, followed by a street dance and fireworks.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  12. Hiawatha Traditional Music Festival

    Marquette, MI2023-07-23

    This folk-music festival in Tourist Park also features a juried arts and crafts show and camping on-site.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends Marquette story

  13. Great Folle Avoine Fur Trade Rendezvous

    Danbury, WI2023-07-23

    At this rendezvous at a rebuilt post on the banks of the Yellow River, visit with costumed traders and voyageurs from the fur-trade era and see demonstrations of tomahawk-throwing, black-powder shooting, candlemaking and other skills. There will also be a candy cannon, bead pile and kids' games. 

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  14. Blueberry Festival

    Iron River, WI2023-07-23

    This small town between Duluth and Ashland celebrates its berry harvest with a pie-eating contest, pedal tractor pull, music, fireworks and Sunday parade.

    1. Event website
