<Sunday, July 23, 2023>

  1. Country Thunder Wisconsin Music Festival

    Twin Lakes, WI2023-07-23

    The country-music festival between Lake Geneva and Kenosha features Jon Pardi, Jason Aldean and Keith Urban. There's camping onsite.

    1. Event website

  2. Fiesta Days

    McHenry, IL2023-07-23

    There's a Music Fest, carnival, beach bash, car show and fireworks in Petersen Park the first weekend. The second weekend, there's an art and street fair Saturday and a 1:30 p.m. Sunday parade on Main Street.

    1. Event website

  3. Northwest Water Carnival

    Detroit Lakes, MN2023-07-23

    This big festival celebrates its 87th year with a sand-castle contest, water fights, ship-building (in less than 30 minutes), beer tasting, watermelon feed, bike tour and more. On the last day, there's a big parade at 1 p.m.

    1. Event website

  4. Bavarian Blast

    New Ulm, MN2023-07-23

    In addition to German music, food and beer, this festival at the Brown County Fair Grounds features stein-holding, barrel-rolling and sauerkraut-eating contests, plus a Sunday parade.

    1. Event website
    2. MidwestWeekends story

  5. Rivertown Days

    Hastings, MN2023-07-23

    In this river town south of St. Paul, there will be 25 bands on four stages, plus a carnival, arts & crafts fair and classic-car show.

    1. Event website

  6. Midwest Fire Fest

    Cambridge, WI2023-07-23

    This pottery town east of Madison celebrates the arts fueled by fire, including clay, glass and metal. It includes fire dancing, music from seven bands, demonstrations and food, including wood-fired pizza. Fest-goers can design their own tiles to be created during a hot-iron pour Saturday.

    1. Event website

  7. Air and Water Show

    Milwaukee, WI2023-07-23

    This show, between Bradford and McKinley beaches on the lakefront, features the Blue Angels in the air and ski teams on the water.

    1. Event website
