MidwestWeekends.com Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2008.

This notice describes our privacy policy. By visiting midwestweekends.com, you are accepting the practices described in this privacy policy.

Personal Information We Collect

  • Automatic Information: When you interact with our web site, we receive connection information such as the internet (IP) address your computer uses to connect to the internet, browser type and version, and the operating system. We use this information only to generate anonymous statistics on the usage of the site.
  • E-mail communication: If your computer preference settings permit it, we receive information when you interact with our e-mail newsletters. This information is not personally identifiable and is only used to assess the popularity of different topics. If you do not want to receive e-mail or other mail from us you can opt-out from receiving mailings by using a link at the bottom of every newsletter we send.

Cookies

Cookies are identifiers that we transfer to your computer to allow us to provide a customized experience. The cookies are not used to monitor your browsing habits. You can turn accepting Cookies off in your browser's "Preferences" settings; Please consult your browser's "Help" function to find out how to change the settings in your browser.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with outside parties that do not directly provide services to us.
